Kauai is the oldest and most northern of of the main Hawaiian Islands. At only 550 square miles, Kauai is regarded as the most beautiful Hawaiian island making it perfect for activities and tours. Its nickname is the Garden Island, although it was originally created by volcanoes. Kauai’s mountains and valleys showcase lovely waterfalls and canyons etched into the earth by the continual rainfall. In fact, Kauai has the proud title of being one of the wettest spots on earth! There are an abundance of Kauai activities to do, from the average tourist to the eco-tourist, you can always plan the best of Kauai adventures to suit your vacation needs!
